ABSee

Student Project




Due to fast fashion, clothes are thrown away faster leading to each person wasting atleast 35kg of textiles per year. This causes waste accumulation and pollution. I created card game sets for children as a design solution to this problem. This card game allows children to learn the alphabet through an app that interacts with the product using the Osmo System.


We had to choose a brand that could sponsor this idea as part of the project, and I chose to use Fox Kids & Baby because they are a popular brand in Singapore and they are working towards being more sustainable and do already have a drop-off point for old clothes. To elevate the project, I chose to create a hypothetical collaboration between Fox Kids & Baby and Osmo to create this game.
