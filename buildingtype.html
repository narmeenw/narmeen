Building Type

Student Project




This was a project where the brief was to design a typeface based on a chosen building. I chose Southwark Cathedral in London because it is an architecture style that is not common in Singapore and I thought it would be interesting to explore a new space and style. I used the curves of the stones and the stained-glass windows of the Cathedral as the basis of the design.


All the typefaces designed by the students of the year group was bound into a collaborative zine. The layout for this was done by the course reps, including me.
