SPH Radio

Design Intern


I was a design intern who created ATL (Above the Line) and BTL (Below the Line) collaterals for this radio station at one of Singapore's top media companies. Working collaboratively with the marketing team, I ensured designs aligned with branding and messaging strategies. I produced graphics for a variety of media, including print and digital platforms. I contributed to the creation of marketing campaigns and promotional materials for the programs and events. I developed skills in design software and tools, including Adobe Creative Suite.


Through this internship I gained hands-on experience working in a professional design environment. I designed print and social media ads, spotfiy icons, and google ads to name a few.
