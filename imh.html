It's All In Your Head

Student Project, 2019


The question for this project was how can Graphic Communication be used for social issues? I chose to create this "exploding" informative box that encompasses information about Schizophrenia in hopes that spreading awareness would reduce the stigma surrounding this mental illness.


This box would potentially be produced in collaboration with Institute of Mental Health Singapore, hence it features a badge with a collaborative logo.
